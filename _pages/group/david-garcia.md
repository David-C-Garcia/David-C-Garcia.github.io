## David C. Garcia

<p style="text-align: justify;">
David Garcia is an assitant professor of chemical biomolecular and environmental engineering at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County. Prior to that I was a Senior Research Scientist at DEVCOM CBC and the California Institute of Technology under the mentorship of Matthew Lux and <a href="https://murray.cds.caltech.edu/Main_Page">Richard Murray</a>, respectively. The goal of my work is to develop new strategies that expand our traditional models of biomanufacturing sensors, materials, and molecules by taking a data-driven and multiplexed approach to elucidate the fundamental principles of scalable cell-free biomanufacturing.
</p>

**Research Interests:**  
- Synthetic biology  
- Cell-free systems  
- Biomanufacturing  
- Biocatalysis  
- Sustainable materials
